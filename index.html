Emergency Department

The emergency department is pleased to serve you. The ER department provides initial treatment for a broad spectrum of illnesses and injuries, some of which may be life-threatening and require immediate attention. The OLMCMC-ERD operates for 24 hours a daily with competent and reliable doctors and nurses with 24 hour ambulance service. The ER team with physicians, nurses, and other healthcare professionals provides patients care, monitor health conditions, plan long-term care needs, administer medicine, use medical equipment, perform minor medical operations, advised patients and their families on illness, care and continued care after a hospital stay.

Neonatal Intensive Care Unit

The Neonatal Intensive Care Unit is high specialized care, and the most advanced pediatric treatment, in a gentle nurturing environment and provides 24-hour service. It accepts 27 weeks gestation or less to tern infants requiring Level II and Level III specialized care. The NICU family provides a family-centered approach to care; encompassing the parents and sick infants as a single unit. Thus including the family in all decision making and kept well informed and have complete understanding of all patient care activities and procedures. Other disciplines are involved in the care of the sick neonate including medical staff, respiratory therapy, pharmacy, radiology, infection control and physical therapy.

Diagnostic and Imaging Department

The Diagnostic and Imaging Department in Our Lady of Mt. Carmel Medical Center has been serving the local community. It utilizes most technologically advanced diagnostic and imaging equipment in the area to better serve our patients. Our compassionate and friendly staff are fully trained with certified technologists, board certified radiologists, and board certified nuclear cardiologist.

Read More

Hemodialysis Center

The hemodialysis unit of Our Lady of Mt. Carmel Medical Center provides quality renal replacement therapy to out-patients and/or in-patients with acute and chronic disease. The department has thirty (30) hemodialysis machines. It is located at the upper ground and second floor (hemodialysis extension) of the medical center. We serve our patients six (6) days a week from 5 am to 10 pm for out-patients.

Read More

Operating Room Department

The operating room (O.R.) is a facility within a hospital specializing in different surgical procedures. Patients are being treated inside O.R. through surgical operations under anesthesia. Asides from the surgical procedures OR provides special care which includes IV fluid administration, medications, electro-cautery machine. Under O.R., the hospital has a Recovery Room (R.R.), known as Post Anesthesia Care Unit (PACU). It houses post – operative patients for a minimum of two hours or until the time the anesthesia had wear off. Moreover, there is a Central Supply Room (CSR) inside the theatre, where all the medicines and supplies are readily available once needed.
